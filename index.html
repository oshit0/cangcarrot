Walang Palayok na Walang Kasukat na Tungtong


Corny ko talaga kahit kailan ahhahahahah yung title pilipino proverb yan ginoogle ko eto lumabas:

  1. This proverb means that every person has his perfect match. Be it a life partner, business partner, etc., there is always a person who will compliment another person.
  2. This kasabihan is used by elders to assure a person that there is someone out there that will be perfect for him. This is a good reminder to anyone who feels he/she is lonely that there is someone out there who is perfect for him/her.
